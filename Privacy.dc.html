Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025. This policy explains how Skyline Travel Planner collects, uses and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information you choose to share with us when you request a customized travel quote or contact us. This may include your name, phone number, email address, travel dates, destination preferences and budget.

We do not process any payments on this website, so we never collect card, bank or other financial details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To prepare and share a customized travel itinerary and quotation.
  • To respond to your enquiries over WhatsApp, phone or email.
  • To remember your language preference for a smoother browsing experience.
  • To improve our website and the packages we offer.

3. Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use a minimal set of cookies to make the website work well for you:

Essential Required for the website to function, including remembering your cookie consent choice.
Preferences Remember your selected language (English, Hindi or Gujarati) so you don't have to re-select it.

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies using the banner shown on your first visit. You may also clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

4. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your personal information. We only share details with trusted travel providers (airlines, hotels, transport partners) when it is necessary to arrange the services you have requested.

5. Your Rights

You can ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. Simply contact us using the details below and we will respond promptly.
